• Squirmy Caterpillar Puppet

    • Create 2 caterpillar puppets that inch along like a real caterpillar.

    Supplies:

    • 4 Popsicle Sticks
    • 2 8x11 sheets of card stock paper (or thick paper)
    • Crayons
    • 1 glue stick
    • 4 google eyes (2 pair)
    • 1 pair of safety scissors
    • 1 large cup

    Project Time:

    • 20 minutes prep
    • 5 minutes to dry

    Grownups Note:

    • Grownups help with cutting.

  • 1) Lets get started!

    • Using 1 (one) sheet fold the paper in half horizontally.
    • Cut that fold making two separate rectangles.
    • Fold each rectangle horizontally.
    • This is the body of the puppet.
    • Color the two pieces on both sides to look like the body of a caterpillar.

  • 2) On the move!

    • Fold the two pieces vertically front to back and back to front like an accordion.
    • Lightly glue the top of each popsicle stick on both sides.
    • Place 1 (one) popsicle stick inside each end of the folded paper.
    • Place aside and allow to dry.

  • 3) Put on a smile!

    • Using the bottom of a cup, trace the circle on the other paper.
    • Cut out the circle (Repeat for second puppet).
    • Cut a small triangle in the circle like a slice of pizza from a large pizza pie.
    • Color the face to match the body of the puppet.
    • Stick on google eyes or draw eyes on each side of the face.
    • Lightly glue the head to one end of the body
    • And inch along like a beautiful caterpillar!
