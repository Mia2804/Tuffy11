• Shadow Puppets

    • Create a tuffy-tastic moving shadow puppets

    Supplies:

    • 1 (one) 8x11 sheet of card stock paper (or thick paper)
    • Crayons
    • 2 Straws
    • 1 Pair of safety scissors
    • 2 Folding fasteners
    • 1 Roll of clear tape
    • 1 Cup
    • 1 Dollar Bill (Or equivalent rectangle shape)
    • 1 Ruler
    • 1 Hole Puncher

    Project Time:

    • 30 minutes prep

    Grownups Note:

    • Grownups help with cutting, measuring, and fasteners.

  • 1) Shape it up!

    • At the top left side of the paper, trace the bottom of a cup making a circle.
    • Place dollar bill under the circle making sure it touches.
    • Trace the dollar bill on all sides creating a rectangle.
    • This is the body and head of the puppet.
    • At the center bottom of the rectangle, draw one (1) leg and foot.

  • 2) With open arms!

    • On the right side of the paper draw two (2) rectangles no more then 1 inch wide/3.5 inches long. These are the arms of the puppet.
    • Pick one (1) of the rectangles.
    • Draw a hand on that one (1) rectangle.

  • 3) Places everyone!

    • Lightly spread glue on one (1) side of the popsicle stick.
    • Place your photo at the top.
    • Place the body underneath.
    • Let it dry for 5 Minutes and you’re ready to star in your own puppet show!

  • 4) Cut it out!

    • Cut out all three (3) pieces: Body WITH Head and foot, arm, and arm WITH hand.
    • BE CAREFUL: DO NOT cut off the foot, or head, or hand. They must all stay together.
    • With hole puncher, punch out ONE (1) eye in the head.
    • Like a slice of pizza from a pizza pie, cut out a smile under the eye.

  • 5) Fasten your seatbelts!

    • WITH PARENTS HELP push one fastener through the top of each rectangle arm.
    • Not too close to the edge.
    • Attach the two arm pieces together at the elbow with one fastener.
    • Attach the now complete arm to the body at the shoulder.
    • Twist the fastener to make it easy to move.
    • Fold out fasteners to secure the parts.

  • 6) Makin’ it move!

    • Flip the puppet over.
    • Tape one (1) straw to the hand.
    • Tape one (1) straw to the back of the body extending part of the straw under the foot.
    • This part of the straw is to hold on to.
    • While holding the body straw, move the hand with the other straw.

  • 7) Look at the shadow!

    • Using a shadow puppet stage or just a light and a clear wall move your puppet and use the shadow to put on your own shadow puppet show.
