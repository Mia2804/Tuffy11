• PUPPET STAGE: Tissue Box

    • Great for Popsicle Puppets, Finger Puppets, or Pom Pom Puppets
    • You’ve made your puppet and now you need to perform it. But where?
    • There’s no better place to perform with a puppet then a puppet stage!

    Supplies:

    • 1 Old tissue box (vertical box)
    • 1 (one) 8x11 sheet of card stock paper (or thick paper)
    • Crayons
    • Stickers/ foam stick-ons
    • 1 Glue stick
    • 1 Pair of safety scissors
    • 1 Roll of tape

    Project Time:

    • 20 minutes

    Grownups Note:

    • Grownups help with folding edges and cutting.

  • 1) Color the Backdrop!

    • First ask yourself- “Where does my puppet’s story take place?”
    • Color the 8x11 paper to fit that story.
    • It could be a forest, a castle, or the moon for example.

  • 2) Decorate the stage!

    • WITH GROWNUPS HELP: Choose one long side of the box and cut a hole for your hand
    • Using crayons, stickers, foam stick-ons decorate the remaining three sides of the tissue box

  • 3) Curtain Up!

    • Place the paper on a table horizontally.
    • Place tissue box on bottom of the 8x11 paper.
    • Center the box on the paper.
    • With a crayon, lightly mark the edges of the box on both sides.
    • Remove the box.
    • WITH GROWNUPS HELP: Fold the paper vertically at the lines.
    • Tape the 8x11 paper to the back of the tissue box with the folded edges around the sides of the box without covering the hole for your hand.
    • Make sure all sides are taped.

  • 4) Show Time!

    • Place your hand through the hole.
    • Place the puppet on your hand.
    • ...and on with the show!
