• Puppet Stage: Table Top Puppet Stage

    • Great for all puppets!
    • You’ve made your puppet and now you need to perform it. But where?
    • There’s no better place to perform with a puppet then a puppet stage!

    Supplies:

    • 1 Large Box
    • Various pieces of construction paper
    • Crayons
    • Stickers/ foam stick-ons
    • 1 Glue stick
    • 1 Pair of safety scissors
    • 1 Roll of masking tape
    • 1 White plastic table cloth
    • 1 Flashlight

    Project Time:

    • 30-40 minutes

    Grownups Note:

    • Grownups help with cutting and measuring.

  • 1) Building your theatre

    • Seal all parts of the box with tape including inside flaps.
    • WITH GROWNUPS HELP cut out two windows on the box.
    • One (1) in front and one (1) in back.

  • 2) Decorate the theatre

    • Using color paper, stickers, and foam cut-outs decorate the outside of the box.

  • 3) Curtain Up!

    • Pick a window for the front and one for the back.
    • The front window is where you will perform, the back is where you enter and hide.
    • Lay the box down with the front window looking up.
    • Place the table cloth on top of the box.
    • Measure the front window of the theatre and cut the plastic table cloth to size up with the window.
    • Place the box right side up again.
    • WITH GROWNUPS HELP Tape the now sized and cut table cloth inside the window.
    • Make sure it is taped tightly, no not let it sag.

  • 4) Show Time!

    • v.
    • BE CAREFUL: DO NOT cut off the foot, or head, or hand. They must all stay together.
    • Have a fellow puppeteer hold the flashlight behind your shadow puppet.
    • ...and on with the show!
    • Take turns performing
    • Pull the plastic away to create a theatre for your other puppets!
