• Popsicle Photo Puppets

    • Create a tuffy-tastic puppet starring YOU!

    Supplies:

    • 1 photo of you and/or family/friends
    • 1 (one) 8x11 sheet of card stock paper (or thick paper) Crayons
    • 1 glue stick
    • 1 popsicle stick
    • 1 pair of safety scissors

    Project Time:

    • 20 minutes prep
    • 5 minutes dry time

    Grownups Note:

    • Grownups help with cutting

  • 1)You’re the star!

    • Pick out a great photo of you. One that is clear and shows you big Tuffy-smile.
    • WITH GROWNUPS HELP: Cut out just your head. Be careful not to cut off your ears!

  • 2) Dress the part!

    • Place your newly cut out photo on the paper.
    • Keeping in mind the size of the photo, draw a body.
    • From the neck to the toes draw your arms, legs and your Tuffy-tastic outfit.
    • Color your puppet’s body using crayons.

  • 3) Places everyone!

    • Lightly spread glue on one (1) side of the popsicle stick.
    • Place your photo at the top.
    • Place the body underneath.
    • Let it dry for 5 Minutes and you’re ready to star in your own puppet show!
