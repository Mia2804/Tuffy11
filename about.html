About Puppetry Arts New York

Welcome to Puppetry Arts New York! Home of Tuffy Tiger!

The mission of Puppetry Arts New York is to offer creative and cultural exploration to all individuals through the multidisciplinary art of puppetry by providing a venue for emerging artists, innovative teachers, and community members to facilitate youth development, enrich family activity, empower civic responsibility, and to engage and entertain.

As a 501c3 non profit, Puppetry Arts New York provides programming at community events, family programs, and schools in the New York City area including artist in residencies, performances, events, and volunteer opportunities.
